The Beatrice Public Library

Mission:

The Beatrice Public Libraray is a nutruring environment that strives to provide unfettered access to

necessary resources for self-improvement, cultural awareness, and pure enjoyment for patrons. We

encourage life-long learning for all.

Vision

BPL is a warm, comforable, welcoming, user-friendly place for people of all ages. It is available to the

entire community, providing free guidance, cultural events, and access to technology, books, and

media. We are here to help you become the best version of you, that you have always imagined.
